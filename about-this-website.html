About This Website

Welcome to the CCRA website! This website has gone through a number of revisions over the years. The original site was made in Wordpress and served the CCRA well for many years. In late 2022, the CCRA decided to completely redesign the website in favor of a simpler, brighter, and easier-to-use website.

The original redesign was created in Fall 2022 for the final project of INFO 1300, an introductory web design/development class at Cornell. In December 2022, the site was further optimized using Bootstrap. Reid Fleishman is the current maintainer of the website and it is hosted on his server.
