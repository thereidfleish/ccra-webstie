Meetings

To get involved in the club, the best way is to come to a meeting!

We have finished our weekly Sunday meetings in Rice Hall for the Spring 2025 semester, but please reach out below if you'd like to request anything or need anything fixed, especially after the Repair Fair! We will be hosting meetings in Gates Hall as-needed throughout the remainder of the Spring 2025 semester.

Starting in Fall 2025, will be moving from Rice Hall and meeting in Gates Hall, times TBD during the academic year, not including breaks.

On any given day, we will be wiping hard drives, reinstalling Windows, replacing computer parts, troubleshooting issues, packing computers for donation, helping a community member fix their device, and more! All are welcome, no experience is necessary and we will teach you everything you need to know!
