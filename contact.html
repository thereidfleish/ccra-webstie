Meetings

To get involved in the club, the best way is to come to a meeting! We meet weekly on Sundays at 1:00 pm on the first floor of Rice Hall starting February 11th, 2024 during the academic year, not including breaks. To find us, enter through the back entrance of Rice Hall and make a left.

All are welcome, no experience is necessary and we will teach you everything you need to know!
